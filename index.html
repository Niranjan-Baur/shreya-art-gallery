About Me

Art is everywhere, like the way you talk, walk,the way you think and everything you see around you like the whole nature - trees, rivers, bees, lake, flower, house etc every single thing is art. The whole world, universe is art. And we highlight those things through a lot of things such as singing, dancing, painting, writing etc. As an example, we can portray a flower through those art forms. We can also describe our emotions, express fear, anger, sadness or happiness. There are so many songs, dance forms, paintings and writings which we can relate to ourselves. And when you can relate something to your own world then those feelings became valuable to you.

I have an affection with painting. I like to draw various things. When I feel low, sad, I paint. I draw when I'm super excited about something. I truly feel that painting reduces stress, anxiety, depression. As I mentioned before that emotions can be expressed through painting. The famous artists Vincent Van Gogh was illustrate his personal expression through his painting such as, Starry Night. Another master artist Pablo Picasso's paintings show us all the aspects of life, sorrow, joy, pleasure, darkness etc. There are many more famous artists are there who painted from their life. Designs, details of every shades, colours, meanings, all this has stole my mind and heart long ago. That's why I love to paint. And I will paint as long as I can, because painting has become a part of my life and I cannot live without it.

Well I'm Shreya Manna, studying English Honour's at Sarsuna college, under Culcutta University and certified uptill 7th year of drawing from Nikhil Bharat Sangeet Samiti. I love watching movies. I like every genre such as adventurous, mysterious, detective, fantasy, action, crime thriller etc. Painting and watching movies take me to another world which is a kind of escapism from the harsh reality. I am good with people and always listen before I talk. I'm also very emotional and sensitive which bothers me sometime but I overcome it with my painting act. I have some interest in writing and reading books, novels, short stories etc. That's all.
